About this activity:

With 13 titles, the Pinheiros Club is the club that has more trophies of the Championship Absolute Swimming . Currently, parade their swimmers swim as Bruno Fratus, Felipe France, Guilherme Guido, among others. One of the most traditional forms of the Club, the secret of success of "Pinheirense" Swimming is the investment in the base and as a result has the category as the strongest and most promising of Brazil.
